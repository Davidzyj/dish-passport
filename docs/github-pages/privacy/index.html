Dish Passport

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026

Dish Passport is a local-first iPhone app. It does not require an account, does not include ads, does not use analytics SDKs, and does not track users.

Data Collection

The app does not collect personal data. Saved dishes, liked dishes, want-to-try items, recently viewed items, and language preference are stored locally on your device.

Network Access

The app does not actively send data to a server. Privacy Policy and Support links open in your browser only when you tap them.

Third Parties

The app does not share data with third parties and does not use third-party tracking.

Contact

For privacy questions, email jay212315@gmail.com.
